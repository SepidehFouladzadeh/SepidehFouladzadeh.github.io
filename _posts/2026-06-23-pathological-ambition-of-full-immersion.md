A modern longing for the furthest depth of immersion.

Perhaps to escape realization, perhaps to inhabit brief moments of idealization, perhaps simply for a safe exploration.

An investment in the virtual experience of full immersion. An augmentation, a technological saturation, a synthetic immersion.

Yet, perhaps overlooking a more authentic immersion. Introspection.

The direction of attention over the manipulation of perception, and perhaps, not relying on vision dominating proprioception. 

No head-mounted ornament for a mediated immersion, but rather a self-generated immersion.

Possessing the organic infrastructure of imagination, yet a maximized immersion, an omnidirectional sensation.

An unexpected complication. 

A new face of full immersion, perhaps cycles of endless reflection. Of what justifies, and what justifies the justifications. 

Ironically, no final rational foundation waiting beneath the excavation.

Paralyzed by excessive reflection. Wrestling with the old philosophical tension of endless contemplation, and delayed engagement.

Excelling at challenging meaning, falling short of its generation.

And a challenge that was perhaps never achieving full immersion, but preserving enough restraint to return.
